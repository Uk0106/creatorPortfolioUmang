Colour No. 04 — Bangalore

Bangalore
in Pink

For two short weeks a year, the tabebuia takes over. Flyovers, traffic, glass towers — all of it softened under a canopy that turns the whole city into something briefly, gloriously pink. Blossom time in Bangalore is not a calendar event. It's a mood.
